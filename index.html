Introduction

For the One-Hundreth anniversary of the Boy Scouts of America, four historic Merit Badges were reactivated. One of these is Signaling. In order to help the members of my brother’s Boy Scout Troop, I wrote this application to assist in learning semaphore signals. It can also be used by a counselor as a prompter for proficiency tests.

Get It

You can use the trainer online, or download the whole bundle for offline use. If you download the offline version, unzip the archive, and open the semaphore.html file in your web browser.
